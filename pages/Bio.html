Who Is Gandhi?

Mahatma Gandhi was a massive deal for the Indian independence movement against British rule. Born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar, India, he is known for fighting for freedom without violence. His efforts led to India achieving independence in 1947, but he was assassinated on January 30, 1948, by Nathuram Godse, a Hindu nationalist.
